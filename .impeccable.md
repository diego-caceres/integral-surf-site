## Design Context

### Users
Mix of beginner and experienced surfers, locals and international travelers (primarily Spanish-speaking, Latam/Uruguay). Browse on both mobile and desktop. Context: planning surf trips or seeking a soul-aligned outdoor experience. They want to feel inspired and trust the brand before booking.

### Brand Personality
**Soulful · Wild · Crafted** — organic, handmade aesthetic. Feels like an independent surf zine or journal. The brand integrates surfing, yoga, art, and nature into a conscious lifestyle. Not a tourist product — an experience.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Palette**: Dark forest green `#0E1A1B` (primary), soft warm beige `#EAD9C9` (secondary), earthy orange-red `#E4572E` (accent), crimson red `#FF3131` (highlight/price)
- **Typography**: Custom Eckmannpsych (display/hero), Playfair Display (serif headings), Bebas Neue (labels/tracking), Libre Franklin (body)
- **Tone**: Editorial, cinematic, unhurried. Wide negative space. Photography-led. Typography that feels hand-lettered.
- **Theme**: Light mode. Earthy, natural, analog-feeling.
- **Anti-references**: Generic tour operators, flashy booking platforms, corporate surf schools

### Design Principles
1. **Photography first** — Images carry the emotional weight; UI should frame them, not compete
2. **Unhurried rhythm** — Generous spacing; scrolling should feel like turning pages in a magazine
3. **Handcrafted over polished** — Prefer organic asymmetry, custom type, and textural contrast over perfectly aligned grids
4. **Clarity of intent** — Every CTA should feel earned and purposeful; avoid visual noise
5. **Mobile is primary** — Most users discover and explore on phone; the experience must feel native, not shrunk-down
